The Process

01

Discovery

Understanding your creative vision, audience expectations, and technical constraints. What are you actually trying to make people feel? What would make this unforgettable?

02

Architecture

Designing systems that balance narrative depth with operational resilience. Character memory structures, interaction flows, cost optimization, and graceful degradation when things go wrong.

03

Build

Rapid iteration on character behavior, interaction patterns, and system integration. Testing with real users early and often. Building the feedback loops that make characters feel alive.

04

Deploy

Getting it live with proper monitoring, fallback systems, and cost controls. Setting up escalation paths for when the AI needs human backup. Making sure your team can manage it.

05

Monitor

Tracking both technical metrics (latency, costs, errors) and subjective qualities (character authenticity, audience engagement). Iterating on behavior based on what actually resonates.
