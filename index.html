The Integration Challenge

Most AI-driven experiences break where creative vision meets technical reality. You get vampires that sound like breakfast mascots, puzzle rooms that crash after ten players, or installations that dazzle in demos but collapse under real crowds, real time pressure, and real budgets.

I solve the junction where story, systems, and operations need to work together.

From Early Experiments to Production Infrastructure

I started with the first ChatGPT and DALL-E APIs, building experimental web games that generated room descriptions and procedural doors on the fly when everyone was still figuring out what was possible.

Today, I build production infrastructure like AI systems that automatically route between 100+ models to balance cost, performance, and personality consistency. When my AI radio station needs to stay cost-effective while running 24/7, it's using tools I built from operational necessity.
